Send files from Dropbox to Kindle
=================================
Automation makes things easy
-----------------------------
You can send file from Dropbox to Kindle, Just put files in your Dropbox and they will be sent to your Kindle.

Here are few steps:-



### Setup Send to Kindle by E-mail
You can send documents to Kindle by sending them to an email address Amazon provices, but you have to set this up. [Learn how](http://www.amazon.com/gp/sendtokindle/email)



### Use IFTTT to send document to your Kindle email address from Dropbox. Here is the Recipe:-
